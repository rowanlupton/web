I am a morris dancer, a contra and english dancer, a singer, a tech geek, a sound engineer, a transfeminine radically feminist human. I care about the underlying fabric of society, and I believe in interacting with the world in constructive ways whenever possible. If you aren't on board with an intersectional feminist outlook, I may not be the right match for you.

My journey with development began before I can remember, certainly before leaving elementary school. My first memory around the subject is asking my father (an astrophysicist, who primarily writes code for telescopes) to teach me how to write a video game, at which time he tried to teach me some Python with a visual library - predictably, my attention span did not last.

I take a client-first approach to design and development; your desires are more important than my personal aesthetics. If I were a web library, I would be unopinionated. Client-based web development is fundamentally a service job, and I have an excellent relation to my clients.
